Our Approach

Safety First

AMSCO is committed to maintaining the highest standards of Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) across all our operations. We believe that all accidents are preventable, and that a strong safety culture is the foundation of operational excellence.

Our safety management system aligns with international standards and the requirements of our major clients including Saudi Aramco. Every employee, contractor, and partner is expected to uphold our safety values and report unsafe conditions without hesitation.

🛡
Zero Harm Commitment

AMSCO's primary objective is zero harm to people, the environment, and assets. We believe all incidents are preventable through proper planning, training, and execution.

📋
Risk Assessment

All work activities are subject to thorough risk assessment and Job Safety Analysis (JSA) before commencement. Hazard identification is a continuous process.

🏫
Training & Competency

All personnel receive regular safety training and competency assessments. We ensure our workforce is qualified, certified, and fit for the tasks they perform.

🟩
PPE Standards

Appropriate Personal Protective Equipment is mandatory for all site personnel. AMSCO supplies high-quality specialized PPE to meet the demands of industrial environments.

🚨
Incident Reporting

All incidents, near-misses, and unsafe conditions are reported and investigated. Lessons learned are shared across the organization to prevent recurrence.

♻
Environmental Protection

AMSCO is committed to minimizing environmental impact through responsible waste management, hydro jetting services, and compliance with environmental regulations.

Our HSE Commitments

Safety Commitments

✅ Compliance

Full compliance with Saudi Arabian labor laws, environmental regulations, and the HSE requirements of our clients including Saudi Aramco Contractor Safety standards.

✅ Continuous Improvement

Continuous review and improvement of our safety management systems, procedures, and practices based on industry best practices and lessons learned.

✅ Employee Empowerment

Every employee has the right and responsibility to stop any work they believe is unsafe. Safety concerns are welcomed and acted upon at all levels of the organization.

✅ Client Standards

AMSCO aligns its safety practices with the standards of our clients, including Aramco's Contractor HSE requirements, to ensure seamless and safe operations on all sites.
