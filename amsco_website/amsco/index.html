About AMSCO

Your Trusted Industrial Partner

AMSCO is an established industrial supplier and service provider based in Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia, with approximately 10 years of proven experience in the local market. The company serves major industries including Oil & Gas, Railway Transportation, and Industrial Fabrication with the financial strength to handle large-scale projects.

Our Vision

To be the top performing and most admired industrial supplier and support services provider in the region, recognized for excellence and reliability.

Our Mission

Integrating advanced technologies, understanding client needs, and believing that client success equals AMSCO success through innovative industrial solutions.

Our Facility

State-of-the-art 12,000 sqm fabrication shop in the 2nd Industrial City, Dammam, with 10-ton crane capacity and full-spectrum manufacturing capabilities.
