Infrastructure

Core Capabilities

AMSCO's physical infrastructure and operational capabilities underpin every project we deliver — from large-scale fabrication to logistics and business support.

12,000 Sqm Fabrication Shop

State-of-the-art fabrication facility in the 2nd Industrial City, Dammam — with full welding, sand blasting, coating, and painting capabilities.

2,000 Sqm Warehouse

Dedicated warehousing and chemical storage laboratory enabling efficient logistics, procurement management, and just-in-time supply delivery.

10-Ton Crane Capacity

Heavy lifting and material handling capabilities within our fabrication shop for efficient production of large industrial components and equipment.

Skilled Workforce

A specialized team spanning engineering, fabrication, electrical, logistics, and administration — with Saudization and IKTIVA compliance built into our staffing model.
